--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STUDIO CRM - USER GUIDE & QUICK START
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1: LOGIN & DASHBOARD
- Launch the CRM from your landing page.
- The Dashboard shows your appointments for "Today" and live revenue summaries.
- Use the Quick Action buttons (New Appointment, Add Client) to save time.

STEP 2: MANAGING CLIENTS
- Go to the 'Clients' tab.
- Add new clients with their email and phone.
- IMPORTANT: Always check for existing profiles before creating new ones to 
  maintain accurate service history.

STEP 3: RECORDING PAYMENTS
- After a session, click "Record Payment" on the Dashboard.
- Select the client and payment method (Cash, Card, Transfer).
- Payments are automatically synced to the 'Financial' tab for your reports.

STEP 4: TEAM & ROLES (ADMINS ONLY)
- Navigate to "Team Management" in the sidebar.
- Add Artists, Piercers, or Receptionists.
- The system generates unique, secure credentials for each staff member.
- Monitor your team's live activity from the Dashboard Overview.

STEP 5: STUDIO GALLERY
- Upload your best tattoos and piercings to the shared Studio Gallery.
- Tag your work (e.g., #realism, #gold) for easy searching.
- Use the "Share" buttons to instantly send portfolio pieces to clients via Email, Facebook, or WhatsApp.

STEP 6: CLIENT PROFILES
- Click the "Action" (Eye Icon) next to any client in the list.
- View comprehensive history, medical alerts, and contact details.
- Use the context-aware "Book New" button to schedule appointments instantly for that specific client.

STEP 7: INVENTORY ALERTS
- Check the 'Inventory' tab daily.
- Items highlighted in RED have fallen below their reorder point.
- Use the "Add Item" button to restock needles, ink, or jewelry.

STEP 8: COMPLIANCE LOGS
- Each morning, use the "Health Check" button in the 'Compliance' tab.
- Log your station sanitization and autoclave tests.
- These records are legally required and securely saved in the Compliance Logs.

PRO TIP:
Use "Dark Mode" in the Settings tab for a high-contrast experience during 
technical sessions in low-light environments.
