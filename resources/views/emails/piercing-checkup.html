It's been two weeks since you got your piercing! Time for a quick check-in on your healing progress.

2-Week Healing Check:

  • Is the swelling going down?
  • Are you still experiencing any pain or discomfort?
  • Is there any discharge (clear/white is normal, yellow/green is not)?
  • Have you been able to clean it twice daily?

Aftercare Reminders:

  • Continue cleaning with sterile saline solution 2x daily
  • Don't touch, twist, or remove your jewelry
  • Avoid sleeping on the pierced side if possible
  • Keep hair products, cosmetics, and perfumes away from the piercing
  • No swimming pools, hot tubs, or lakes for another 2-4 weeks

Contact Us If You Notice:

  • Increased redness, heat, or swelling
  • Yellow or green discharge with a bad odor
  • Jewelry feels too tight or is sinking into the skin
  • Bumps forming around the piercing
  • Severe pain that's getting worse

Need a Jewelry Change or Downsizing?

Some piercings benefit from having the jewelry downsized after initial swelling goes down (usually around 4-6 weeks). If you think you're ready, or if something doesn't feel right, stop by the studio or reply to this email to book an appointment.
